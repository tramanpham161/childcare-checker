Work & Income

We need to know your employment status to calculate your eligibility for free hours.

Includes self-employment or being on leave (e.g., maternity).

This is based on your net income after tax. It's roughly the equivalent of working 16 hours a week at National Minimum Wage.

help

If you’re on paid leave (such as maternity or sick leave), you can still be eligible even if your income is currently below the threshold.
